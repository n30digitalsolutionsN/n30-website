**Response:** "I hear you. Most people think websites are K10,000+. Ours start at K299. We're structured specifically for small businesses and startups. Let me show you what we can do at your price point."

### Objection: "I Don't Think I Need A Website"
**Response:** "That's actually common. But here's the thing - 80% of your customers are probably searching for you online right now. If they can't find you, they're finding your competitors. A website is your 24/7 salesperson. It's actually cheaper than traditional marketing."

### Objection: "I Don't Have Time To Manage It"
**Response:** "Perfect. We handle the technical side. We can even manage updates for you. Your job is running the business. Our job is making sure customers can find you online."

### Objection: "I'm Interested But Let Me Think About It"
**Response:** "Totally understand. How about this - let me send you a quick proposal so you can see the exact costs. We can chat again in a week. And if you have questions, just call me. No pressure."

### Objection: "What If It Doesn't Get Me Customers?"
**Response:** "That's fair to ask. Here's what we do: we build websites optimized for search (SEO), we make them mobile-friendly so people can contact you, and we help you with content that attracts your ideal customers. We also provide 3+ months of support. We're not just building it and leaving. We're invested in your success."

### Objection: "I Need To Talk To My Partner/Manager/Family"
**Response:** "Perfect. What if I send you a proposal and a few testimonials from similar businesses? That way, you have something concrete to discuss. Then when you're ready, we can move forward. Sound good?"

## üìû PHONE CALL FLOW

**1. Greeting (1 min):**
"Hey [Name]! Thanks for calling. How are you doing? Great! So tell me - what's your business about?"

**2. Listen (5 min):**
Let them talk. Take notes. Ask follow-up questions.

**3. Qualify (2 min):**
"Sounds interesting. Quick question - do you currently have a website? How's that working for you?"

**4. Pitch (3 min):**
"Here's what I'm thinking... based on what you said, I think we can help. We've done similar work with [Butibam Mantics]. Here's what we'd do..."

**5. Ask (1 min):**
"Does that sound interesting to you?"

**6. Next Steps (2 min):**
If yes: "Great! Let me send you a proposal and we'll move forward."
If no: "No problem. Let me send you some resources and we can chat in a couple weeks."
